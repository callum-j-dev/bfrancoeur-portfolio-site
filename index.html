From the fall: perennial cutbacks and pruning; to the summer: weeding and mulching; to the spring tidy: debris clean-up, seed collection & seeding, transplanting, and planting. Ongoing maintenance is what helps a garden thrive, adapting properly to our space, and put on a show every year.

I enjoy designing functional spaces with native plantings and naturalized design. I use this style to help blend the planting and your home with the New York City Landscape, natural & manmade. Plantings are a beautiful way of realizing and accentuating the potential of our outdoor spaces. I enjoy working with people to help identify the primary uses currently or desired of their outdoor space and design accordingly.
