Maintenance

Garden maintenance is so so so so so so so so important. Garden maintenance allows the intended design to take shape. From the spring perennial cutbacks and pruning; to the summer weeding and mulching; to the fall tidy with debris clean-up, seed collection & seeding, transplanting, and planting. Ongoing maintenance is what helps a garden thrive, adapt to its setting, and put on a show every year.

I am well equipped to handle all tasks of garden maintenance, with a specialty in pruning shrubs and trees properly. I only work on trees and shrubs that can be pruned from the ground or a small ladder and am happy to consult.


Other tasks include

  • Plant Identification
  • Perrenial Cutbacks
  • Pruning
  • Weeding
  • Mulching
  • Soil Amendment
  • Debris Clean-Up
  • Seed Collection & Seeding
  • Transplanting
  • Plant Divisions
  • Planting
  • Bulbs
